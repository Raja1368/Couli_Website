About Me

Welcome to Coulko & Feres! With a passion for logistics and a commitment to excellence, I specialize in transporting a wide variety of goods from America to Africa. From cars and furniture to appliances and more, I ensure that your items reach their destination safely and efficiently. Over the years, I've built a reputation for trustworthiness and reliability. My goal is to make the shipping process as seamless and stress-free as possible for my clients. Whether you're moving household items, shipping a vehicle, or transporting commercial goods, I am here to provide you with top-notch service. I understand the importance of timely deliveries and the care required for handling diverse types of cargo. That's why I use the latest technologies and adhere to the highest industry standards to protect your goods throughout their journey. Thank you for considering Coulko & Feres for your shipping needs. I look forward to serving you and helping your items reach their new home in Africa.
